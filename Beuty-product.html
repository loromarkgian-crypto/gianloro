Glow Skincare Campaign

Final Posters

Overview

The Glow Skincare Campaign is a refined beauty advertising concept designed to evoke softness, calm, and everyday luxury. The objective was to create elegant, minimal posters that communicate gentle care, clean ingredients, and natural radiance in fast-scrolling digital environments.

The Objective

  • Capture attention with soft elegance
  • Communicate gentleness and skin-friendly care
  • Highlight clean, nourishing ingredients
  • Encourage subtle but clear call-to-action

Creative Direction

Radiance-Focused Visuals

Each product — cleanser, cream, and serum — was treated as the hero element. Floating compositions and soft shadows create depth while maintaining a clean, modern aesthetic.

Soft Color Palette

Blush pinks and warm neutral tones were intentionally selected to reflect comfort, care, and natural beauty while ensuring brand consistency.

Elegant Typography

Refined serif headlines such as “Glow Gently, Glow Daily” enhance the premium feel while maintaining clarity and visual balance.
