Chocolate Moch Campaign

Final Posters

Overview

The Chocolate Moch Campaign is a premium confectionery advertising concept designed to evoke indulgence, warmth, and sensory desire in fast-scrolling digital environments. The objective was to create visually immersive posters that stimulate craving instantly while maintaining a cohesive and elegant brand identity across multiple flavor variations.

The Objective

  • Capture attention in digital feeds within seconds
  • Trigger sweet cravings through rich visual storytelling
  • Highlight distinct flavor variations clearly
  • Communicate indulgence and premium quality
  • Encourage purchase intent

Creative Direction

Flavor-Driven Visuals

Each variation — Dark Chocolate, Strawberry, and Mocha — was treated as a unique visual experience. Supporting ingredients such as chocolate bars, fresh strawberries, and coffee beans reinforce flavor identity while keeping the product pouch as the hero element.

Strategic Color Psychology

Deep browns, soft pinks, and warm beige tones were intentionally selected to reflect richness, sweetness, and comfort. The dripping chocolate motif creates consistency while enhancing indulgence appeal.

Bold Yet Elegant Typography

Oversized “moch” typography establishes strong brand presence, while the script-style “Chocolate” adds softness and warmth. The combination creates a refined yet scroll-stopping visual hierarchy.
