Burger Campaign

Final Posters

Overview

The Burger Campaign is a high-impact food advertising concept designed to capture attention in fast-scrolling digital environments. The objective was simple: create bold, visually striking posters that instantly stimulate appetite while delivering strong promotional messaging.

The Objective

  • Instantly attract attention
  • Amplify appetite appeal
  • Communicate energy and excitement
  • Encourage immediate action

Creative Direction

Appetite-Driven Visuals

The burger was treated as the hero element with detailed ingredient layering to enhance realism and desirability.

Strategic Color Use

Warm tones such as yellow and orange were selected for their psychological association with hunger and urgency.

Bold Typography

Oversized headlines like “CAN YOU EAT ME?” add personality and visual impact while maintaining clarity.
